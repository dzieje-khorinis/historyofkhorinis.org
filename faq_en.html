  • What is the History of Khorinis?

    The project is a game prequel, 10 years before the plot of the game and BEFORE the creation of the Magic Barrier. Everything in that project is non-profit, so it means that we do not collect any money for our work. Thanks to courtesy of friends we have access to professional sound development studios.

    The entire project is characterized by maximum compatibility with the world of Gothic game

    Before reading, watch the videos on the channel, this is the most important:

  • When the History of Khorinis will be released?

    Unfortunately, we CANNOT estimate the release date at this very moment. We are working hard, polishing every details and there are 50 actors left to prepare voice-overs. We are arranging with the recording studios – which are help us in our project for free - individually. The same thing goes with all people, who are helping us. The problem is that a big company can rent a recording studio and hire actors for a month. So it is IMPOSSIBLE to estimate the one true release date, because NOBODY has ever made a project on big scale in a non-profit way.

  • Will the addon be paid/free?

    The addon will be COMPLETELY free and it will be available to download from our website.

  • Do I need a original Gothic to play?

    Yes. You must have a Gothic 2 Night of the Raven.

  • Does Piranha Bytes know of this?

    Yes, they liked our official Facebook profile!

  • On what stage is the production of the addon?

    Our addon is in the condition of putting all elements together – we are rewriting scripts into game code, placing the characters to the finished world and adding the voice-overs instantly on the same stage. We do not throw any release dates, because we do not know it will last two months, or eight. There are approximated date, but there are many things, which are impossible to estimate.

  • Will there be any graphical improvements?

    We want that even the players with low-end hardware could have a fun from our addon, so that’s why we are going to put a possibility to install DirectX 11 wrapper into the addon installer. Moreover, we are creating our production, to make look minimally better on the “normal” Gothic, but we are also creating a graphic, which cooperates with DirectX 11, so it allows to make better effects.

  • For how many years has the project been in development?

    A concept to the History of Khorinis came to mind in 2008, but the project was supposed to look different than now. There is practically NOTHING from that period what could be used now. The History of Khorinis, which is currently in development, is being made from practically ZERO to ALMOST 2 years! Currently we are on the best way to finish the project.

  • Will we translate addon to other languages?

    Yes, we're planning to in-house translate addon and we'll share translating tools for everyone who want to help us. At this moment we can confirm translations to Polish, English, German, Russian and Italian.

  • Do you already have experience with similar projects?

    Some of the team members are working or worked in the largest Polish and global IT, movie and electronic companies such as Microsoft , Nokia , Autodesk or Polish Filmmakers Association

  • Can I play in Steam/GOG?

    Yes. Every Gothic version, including Steam/GOG, is supported.

  • What will be the factions/guilds?

    City Guard and Merchant Guild Mercenary . Mages will not be a faction you can join.

  • World

  • How big is the new island compared to the Khorinis island?

    Both, the island and city of Vaduz are around 2x the size of their equivalents on Khorinis.

  • Will there be any snow biomes on Vaduz?

    No, the island is all green ;)

  • What about the castle in the Valley of Mines?

    The castle will be restored to its former glory, no more broken towers!

  • Will there be any mages' monastery on the island?

    Adanos is worshiped on Vaduz, so there is a monastery dedicated to him.

  • Will the monastery in the Valley of Mines look slightly more intact?

    Yes, the ruins will be revamped for sure.

  • Will there be a possibility to explore Jharkendar?

    Yes and it will be very well explained. We are not using the portal, of course!

  • What about Inubis' tomb near Onar's farm?

    Probably no major changes will be made to this place.

  • Gameplay

  • How could the path of a mage not be accessible?

    By creating another, separate story path, we would be only stuck with additional work on balancing, that would only delay the release of our mod.

  • How many hours of gameplay will be there?

    Around the same as it was in G2 with its addon.

  • Will one be able to become a mage/necromancer?

    No, the only magic available to the player is going to be scrolls.

  • Will the looks of the character change as he's getting stronger?

    No.

  • Are you planning to add dual wielding to the mod?

    No, there will only be ashield combat system called "USWT" (pol. Universal Shield Fighting System), made by our friend Gother.

  • How will the cooking system work?

    Converting ingredients like berries, cheese, honey, apples, meat and others into bigger dishes that restore more HP (alternative for potions). Recipes will be found throughout the game.

  • Are NPCs going to react/comment our actions in the game?

    Of course!

  • What about ore mining? It looked really unfinished in G2.

    There will be a lot of things to mine in our mod!

  • Will there be any quests started by; e.g. reading a note in some mine?

    Yes, you won't be disappointed.

  • Others

  • What are the system requirements?

    We haven't finished yet so it would be unwise to say for sure. The mod will probably need around 2GB of free space, but you will run it on any PC that could handle Gothic 2 NotR.

  • How much space will be needed on hard drive?

    Again - about 2 GB.

  • Will Mud be back?

    For the hundredth time, yes.

  • Will one take part in Lee's drama?

    It's quite sure that these events took place after the creation of the magic barrier, so no.

  • Are new cutscenes going to be added?

    Yes.

  • Will there be pirates?

    Yes.

  • What type of atmosphere will Vaduz take - more of G1, or G2?

    The atmosphere will be very similar to the atmosphere known from G2.

  • Will one be able to hear swear words in new dialogues?

    Nothing more drastic than G1/G2 swearing.

  • Will there be a pleasure house on Vaduz?

    There will be something very similar to the Red Lantern from Khorinis' harbour.

  • Will there be a multiplayer mode in the History of Khorinis?

    No.

  • Is the harbour of Khorinis going to be revived?

    Yes.

  • Will there be women with various professions on Vaduz? Alchemist, sorceress etc.

    There will be no such women for the same reasons as there were no female paladins in G2. There are SOME important women on Vaduz – nothing too radical.

  • Will there be any faction-neutral armour in the mod (like Leather or Crawler Plate ones)?

    The amount of armour will easily exceed 100, counting equipement unavailable to the player.

  • Are you still planning to recreate Gothic Sequel factions?

    Yes. The king's guard, demon hunters and others!

  • Will there be a possibility to join as an apprentice for more masters than it was in G2?

    Yes.

  • Are you planning on adding any new monsters to the game?

    Yes, there will be some 'new' ones, but nothing over the top.

  • Will the old sountracks be included in the mod?

    Yes, old scores will be reprised, but also new music will be created by our talented composers and orchestra.

  • Will hard moral choices be a part of the mod?

    Yes.

  • Are the characters known from G1-G2 going to look rightly younger?

    Yes.

  • Will there be any easter-eggs?

    There will be plenty, not only Gothic-related.

  • Will the paladin order be split into lesser fractions?

    No. There is only one order. Instead, we will be adding feudal knighthood, that was mentioned in G1.

  • All plants and herbs known from G1/G2 will be included?

    Those from G2 + a lot of new ones.

  • What about the poor dexterity balance from G1 and G2?

    A lot of changes are planned, as well as certain guilds connected only to dexterity talent!

  • Will there be any quests related to deities known from Gothic?

    Related to, yes, but no missions given by the gods, as it was in G3.

  • What about the Seekers?

    Only people who couldn't resist the dark influence of Beliar and the Sleeper became them, so they won't be seen in our mod.

  • Are you bringing Vino's distillery into play?

    Yes. Alcohol will be one of the themes in the mod!

  • Will there still be a possibility to steal the Dragon Slayer sword by just rushing into the cave on a speed potion?

    The location will be accordingly changed, so it won't be that easy.

  • Before the fall of the barrier, Khorinis wasn't flooded with bandits. Will there be any of them in the mod?

    The city guard was actually working back then, but there will be a few bandit groups to run across.

  • Will there be any option to change the looks of the character; e.g. haircut or skin tone?

    No.

  • Will silent assassinations be a part of the gameplay?

    It doesn't fit Lars - the hero, nor the plot of the game.

  • Will new magic circles, like air or earth magic, be added?

    We have never heard about them and never will.

  • Will the unused throwing animation from G1 be implemented?

    No, it's unnecessary.

  • Will crafting be a part of the game?

    Yes, but probably only the NPCs will take care of it.

  • Will gravedigging for items be a part of the game?

    Interesting. If time permits - you have it!.

  • Will there be multiple possibilities to finish a mission?

    Yes.

  • Will drawing a weapon in public draw attention to the player?

    Of course, the same way it did in G1/G2.

  • Will there be a possibility to ride a horse?

    No, it is unnecessary.

  • Will we return to Irdorath island?

    There is no such possibility. 40 years before the action of Gothic 2, that is 29 years before the action of our mod, Irdorath and the other four of Beliar temples mysteriously disappeared from Earth, which says Pyrokar.

  • Will there be quests that rely on investigations or such other things?

    Yes, there will be a lot of this type or similar quests.

  • Is the game going to be split into 6 chapters?

    No.
